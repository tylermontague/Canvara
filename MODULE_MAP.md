# Canvara Module Map

*Naming and information architecture for product surfaces. Ratified July 2026.*

## The Headquarters Metaphor

Canvara is the campaign's headquarters. Each module is a room. The rule, in one
sentence: **Labs think, Workshops produce, the Field Office runs the ground game.**

## The Four Rooms

| Module | Loop Stages | Contents | Requirement IDs |
|--------|-------------|----------|-----------------|
| **Canvara Field Office** | Gather (operations) | Walk lists, shifts, canvasser management, turf assignment. The mobile app ("Canvara Field") is the Field Office in the canvasser's pocket. | CC-4, CC-9 (field portions), all FA-* |
| **Canvara Voter Intelligence Lab** | Learn · Model · Measure | Synthesis of ALL intelligence streams per the data hierarchy: first-party conversations (ambient polling), structured survey questions, direct polling, licensed voter file, external polling. Ambient Pulse dashboard, aggregate views, alerts, review queue, calibration. Measure lives here deliberately — outcomes flowing back into the Lab is the closed loop made visible. | CC-1, CC-2, CC-3, IE-* surfaces |
| **Canvara Message Lab** | Craft | Resonance-keyed template library, Narratives feature, moral foundations tagging, narrative alignment scoring, guardrail checks (ceiling-of-persuasion, narrative contamination). A Lab because messages are hypotheses tested against evidence. | CC-5 |
| **Canvara Voter Contact Workshop** | Deliver | Paid & owned media: text, mail, signs, digital, CTV. Channel-mix recommender, delivery platform exports (integrate-don't-own). Door-knocking is NOT here — it lives in the Field Office. | CC-6, CC-7, CC-8 (delivery side) |

## Console Navigation (v1)

Field Office · Voter Intelligence Lab · Message Lab · Voter Contact Workshop · Admin

## Naming Notes

- "Canvara Field" (mobile app) and "Canvara Field Office" (console module) are
  intentionally paired.
- The Intelligence Lab absorbs Measure; do not create a fifth "measurement"
  module. Calibration credibility is a Lab function.
- Canvassing is special because it is simultaneously contact AND collection —
  which is why it anchors its own room rather than being filed under either
  intelligence or media.
